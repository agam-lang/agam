# Phase 26: Quantum and ZKP Primitives

## Status

- Overall status: open

## Goal

- Explore frontier language-native support for quantum and zero-knowledge-proof primitives.

## Main Motive

- Push Agam into future-facing computation domains without reducing those domains to library-only afterthoughts.

## Responsible Crates

- `agam_ast`
- `agam_sema`
- `agam_hir`
- `agam_mir`
- `agam_codegen`
- `agam_macro`
- `agam_smt`

## Planned Work

- Add native `Qubit` types and quantum gate operations.
- Add `#[zkp]`-style compilation support so mathematical logic can lower directly into zero-knowledge-proof workflows.

## What To Build Next

- Treat this as a long-horizon research phase.
- Do not prioritize it ahead of the product-path compiler/runtime work.

## Dependencies And Prerequisites

- strong core language and IR foundations
- dedicated research bandwidth

## Agent Interpretation

- This is a far-future frontier phase, not a near-term product priority.
